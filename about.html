Meet The Team

The backbone of great patient care is great people. With 40 years combined experience, we’re uniquely qualified to find the right talent for your setting. We recruit for clinical, non-clinical, healthcare IT, professional and executive roles.

Stephanie Parker

President, Managing Partner and Executive Recruiter

Stephanie Parker is the founder and managing partner of TalentSpark. She’s spent most of her 20-year recruiting career in healthcare finding talent for clinical, non-clinical, professional and executive roles. Prior to founding the company, Stephanie served as the Recruiting Manager at the largest health system in Idaho. She has her undergraduate degree from the University of Oregon and her graduate degree in Industrial Organizational Psychology from Kansas State. Stephanie enjoys the science of selection and has expertise in personality assessments. She is certified in Hogan Assessments (the only assessment legally validated for selection) and Myers-Briggs Type Indicator® (MBTI®) personality inventory. She loves helping clients find the best talent through the use of data and designing rigorous selection systems that result in optimal hiring outcomes.

Jamie Brajcich, FASPR

Healthcare Practice Recruiter and Account Manager

Jamie Brajcich, TalentSpark’s Healthcare Practice Recruiter, has over a decade of healthcare recruiting experience in multiple western states including Idaho, Montana, Oregon and Colorado. Prior to her role at TalentSpark, she was the Manager of Physician Recruitment at one of the largest health systems in the nation. Jamie is recognized as a Fellow of Association of Staff Physician Recruiting (FASPR) for her expertise in physician recruitment. Jamie received her degree in Communication Studies and Nonprofit Administration from the University of Montana.

Allison Cunningham

VP of Client Relations and Managing Partner

Allison “Allie” Cunningham is TalentSpark's VP of Client Relations and managing partner. She has 15 years of recruiting experience in bioscience/pharma, healthcare and insurance industries including time with several Fortune 500 companies and large health systems. She has successfully managed hiring ramps for several EHR implementations and hospital expansion projects. She has expertise in market research and finding specialized and technical talent. Allie has a passion for healthcare and how good hiring can positively impact patient care. She is a Colorado native, former Division 1 track athlete and graduate of the University of Colorado and now calls Idaho home. When she is not talking about recruiting, you can find her talking about her love for the west.

Lauren Terry

RECRUITER

Lauren Terry is a recruiter and account manager for TalentSpark. Lauren has experience recruiting for the largest health system in Idaho with a focus on rural facilities and clinics. Additionally, she has experience finding talent in IT and the public sector. She is a San Diego native and a graduate of the University of Idaho. Lauren is a passionate recruiting partner who seeks to understand what makes an organization unique and create exceptional experiences for clients and candidates alike.

Jolie Allred

RECRUITER

Jolie Allred is TalentSpark’s recruiter and has experience working in healthcare and health insurance. Jolie is a skilled recruiting project specialist and was instrumental in the execution of two recent hiring projects for TalentSpark, including the hiring of 100 sales professionals nationwide and a major expansion program for the largest health insurance provider in Idaho. She’s a Boise State University alum with a degree in Health Sciences.
